## ❌ Discrepancies
*   **[P1] Environment & Background Art:** The playable features very basic, procedural flat shapes (straight vertical tree trunks, untextured flat ground, plain sky). The source uses a rich, parallaxed jungle environment with clouds, rolling hills, and a textured dirt/grass ground layer. (Video 00:01 vs Frame `clip2_0001.png`)
*   **[P1] HUD & UI Polish:** The playable's top health bar is unstyled canvas UI. It lacks the custom "VS" logo asset, the castle thumbnail icons, and the heavy black stroke/shadow around the percentage text present in the source. (Video 00:00 vs Frame `clip2_0001.png`)
*   **[P1] Aiming Visuals & Cursor:** The playable uses a generic white Web-style pointer and lacks the transparent grey "funnel" graphic behind the dotted line. It also incorrectly adds a floating white triangle indicator above the active character. The source uses a fleshy custom hand cursor and includes a red "X" cancel button asset. (Video 00:07 vs Frame `clip2_0023.png`)
*   **[P1] Projectile Physics & Speed:** When fired in the playable, the white projectiles drop steeply with low velocity, making the resulting arc feel heavy and inaccurate relative to the drawn dotted line. The source implies faster, straighter trajectories. (Video 00:14 vs Frame `clip2_0068.png`)
*   **[P2] Enemy Bomber Entity:** The playable introduces a rigid airplane sprite to drop the initial bomb, whereas the source utilizes an animated flying bird. (Video 00:02 vs Frame `clip2_0001.png`)
*   **[P2] Explosion VFX:** Playable explosions are just basic scaled circles with blurry edges. The source game has distinct, fiery, multi-layered sprite-based explosions. (Video 00:05 vs Frame `clip2_0136.png`)
