## ✅ Matches well
* Core concept is intact: a mechanized, track-mounted castle taking damage, losing exterior walls, and tilting backwards.
* A cross-section interior view exists, detailing vertical platforms containing various stationed troops.
* The trio of character archetypes is reproduced (a Red Cyclops, an undead Skeleton, and a Green Goblin).
* Rain weather effect is present in both implementations.
* Top UI structurally matches the Blue vs. Red layout with respective castle icons and percentage text.

## ❌ Discrepancies
**[P0] Aiming input logic reversed:** The playable ad features a direct-drag interaction (dragging from the character down to target) (00:05). The source game utilizes an Angry Birds-style slingshot/pull-back mechanic where pulling right aims left (Frame `clip2_0023.png`).
**[P0] Missing character weapons:** In the source game, the characters visibly hold heavy cannons/guns matching their class (Frame `clip2_0023.png`). In the playable ad, the characters are completely empty-handed (00:04).
**[P0] Missing aiming cone:** Source game projects a prominent translucent white cone encapsulating the dotted white trajectory line (Frame `clip2_0023.png`). The playable ad only shows a thin dotted line (00:05).
**[P1] Interior wall aesthetics:** The source interior uses sleek, stark black/dark gray bricking for solid silhouettes, and distinct wooden planks for standing platforms (Frame `clip2_0023.png`). The playable ad uses a visually noisy, disjointed yellow-brown block texture with flat orange platforms (00:04).
**[P1] Camera framing & missing opponent:** The source game zooms out to show both castles on screen simultaneously for context (Frame `clip2_0046.png`). The playable ad remains zoomed in on one castle, causing the player to blindly aim off-screen.
**[P1] Background environment mismatch:** The source game features deep, stylized curvy jungle hills and soft clouds (Frame `clip2_0001.png`). The playable ad uses generic flat pine trees and contains a broken, two-toned sky background with a harsh vertical seam dividing blue and green skies (00:04).
**[P1] Base entry doors missing:** Source tracks have rounded, detailed wooden entry doors on the orange lower body (Frame `clip2_0001.png`). The playable ad just leaves dark, empty holes (00:00).
**[P2] General UI styling:** The top text in the source game has heavily stylized white fonts with thick black outlines, bold top health bars, and a custom chunky "VS" logo (Frame `clip2_0001.png`). The playable ad uses thin internal health bars and basic unstroked black text (00:00).
