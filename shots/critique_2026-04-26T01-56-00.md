## ❌ Discrepancies
* **[P0] Missing terrain physics and dynamic tilt** — In the playable, the castles sit perfectly horizontally on a flat ground line (00:04). In the source, the terrain is bumpy and the entire castle tilts on its tracks dynamically to match the slope and recoil (clip2_0046).
* **[P1] Destruction mask punches through to the sky** — When the opponent's tower takes damage in the playable, the destroyed section becomes completely transparent, revealing the trees and sky behind it (00:11). In the source, breaking the outer wall reveals the dark inside back-wall and interior platforms (clip2_0091).
* **[P1] Top UI elements are entirely unstyled** — The playable uses raw system fonts for the percentages and VS text, and flat borderless color rects for the health bars (00:00). The source uses puffy white text with a thick black drop-shadow/outline, and proper UI containers for the health bars (clip2_0001).
* **[P1] Environment background mismatch** — The playable features entirely flat pine trees and simple green ground (00:01). The original game utilizes a much softer, multi-layered vector background consisting of rolling teal/green hills, distant clouds, and a deep brown dirt cross-section (clip2_0001).
* **[P1] Missing impact explosion and damage text** — Hitting the enemy tower in the playable only triggers a small, generic grey smoke puff (00:11). The source includes a massive, fiery yellow/orange explosion with flying debris and floating "-140" damage numbers (clip2_0158).
* **[P1] Tank tracks lack texture/detail** — The treads on the bottom of the castles in the ad look like solid black ovals with tiny dots (00:00). The source features prominent grey gear-cogs and chunky, highly detailed tread pads (clip2_0001).
* **[P2] Missing 'Cancel' UI during aiming** — When the player pulls back to aim, the source material shows a distinct pink/red "X" icon located right behind the character to cancel the shot (clip2_0023). This is missing in the playable (00:08).
* **[P2] Projectile sprite glitch** — In the playable, the projectile leaves the cannon as a small white ball (00:09) but instantly transforms into a large red rocket during the flight sequence (00:10).

## 🔧 Fix list (ordered by impact)
1. **Fix Destruction Alpha Masking:** Adjust the z-index rendering of the canvas so the destruction mask *only* applies to the outer wall sprite container. Place a solid black/dark brick layer (`#111111`) behind it so holes reveal the interior layer, not the sky.
2. **Update UI Rendering:** Overhaul the top UI drawn in JS/Canvas. Use `ctx.lineWidth = 4`, `ctx.strokeStyle = '#000000'`, and `ctx.fillStyle = '#FFFFFF'` for the percentage text. Give the HP bars a 4px black border. 
3. **Add Impact FX & Damage Numbers:** On projectile collision, spawn a particle emitter using bright fire hexes (`#FFD700`, `#FF4500`). Instantiate Canvas text reading "-140", tween its `y` position upward by ~40px, and tween `alpha` to `0` over `800ms`.
4. **Replace Background Art:** Swap the current repeating pine-tree asset for a layered parallax background matching the source's rolling hills (`#82B395` and `#689D78` tones).
5. **Update Track Assets:** Replace the placeholder black tracks with the correct detailed sprite containing the two internal gears and spiked treads. 
6. **Implement Fake Physics/Recoil:** If full terrain tracking is too heavy for Canvas2D, apply a CSS or canvas rotational transform (e.g., `rotate(-6deg)`) to the parent container of the castle when taking heavy damage to simulate the swaying weight of the original.
