# Winner — V1 cold-open

**Selected:** V1 — Cold-open hook (bomb mid-arc, HP 15%, red danger vignette)

## Rationale

Pattern-interrupt in the first 1.5 s is the highest-leverage axis given 2026 UA signal: 71% of retention is decided in the first 2 seconds. V1 collapses the intro from 4.5 s of bomb pre-roll to a mid-arc opening with a massive 85% damage hit and a red radial danger vignette — the viewer is dropped into a "save it NOW" state before they've registered the brand.

## Trade-offs vs alternates

- **V2 aim-preview** — clean clarity lift, but only matters for users who already passed the first-2s gate. Keep as a stackable mod.
- **V3 night-storm** — strong thumb-stop differentiation in feed, no gameplay risk. Best stacked on top of V1.
- **V4 fail-tease endcard** — only kicks in after 40 s; lift hits a smaller funnel. Strong for D1 install rate but not retention@5s.

## Recommended stack for next pass

V1 (hook) + V3 (palette) + V4 (endcard) is mutually compatible: hook handles thumb-stop, palette handles in-feed differentiation, endcard handles redirect. V2 is independent and stack-safe — if engineering bandwidth allows, layer it in.
